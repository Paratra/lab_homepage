The AIL, led by Dr. Peter Kner, specializes in developing
cutting-edge techniques to enhance the resolution of
three-dimensional fluorescence imaging. Our primary
focus is on integrating various super-resolution methods,
such as structured illumination and STORM, with adaptive
optics. This synergy allows us to achieve sub-diffraction
resolution in model organisms, notably C. elegans and
zebrafish.
